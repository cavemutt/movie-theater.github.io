The Elm was once the meeting place of the world's greatest Superheroes. After a time, the film and theater people started moving in, and the superheroes became executive producers. The theater people became brewers, naturally, but as expected drank more than they sold. Now, in honor of the greatness that came before, the heroes of The Elm Draughthouse are dedicated to serving the surrounding communities with tasty food, including the BEST popcorn, yummy beverages for all ages, and some of the world's greatest entertainment!
